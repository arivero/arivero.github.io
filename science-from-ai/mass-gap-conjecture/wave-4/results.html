Results: Mass Gap Conjecture (Wave 4)

Evaluating the area-law expression $W(A)=e^{-\sigma A}$ with $\sigma=0.2$\,GeV$^2$ produced loops $W(1)=0.819$, $W(2)=0.670$, and $W(3)=0.549$, supporting the confinement picture.

The strong-coupling lattice estimate $m=-\ln(\beta/6)/a$ with $\beta=5.7$ and $a=0.1$\,fm yielded a light mass scale $m=0.513$\,fm$^{-1}$ ($0.10$\,GeV), while fitting the propagator $G(r)=e^{-mr}$ to data at $r=0.5$\,fm gave a heavier mass $2.41$\,fm$^{-1}$ ($0.48$\,GeV).

Together these calculations offer complementary bounds on the Yang–Mills mass gap.
