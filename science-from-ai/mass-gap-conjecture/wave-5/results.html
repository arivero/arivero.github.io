Results: Mass Gap Conjecture

The $4\times4$ lattice simulation produced an area law $W(C)\approx e^{-0.18A}$, indicating a string tension of about $0.18$.

Integrating the $2+1$ dimensional beta function gave $g(\mu)=\frac{g_0}{\sqrt{1+\frac{11g_0^2}{6\pi}\ln(\mu/\mu_0)}}$, showing a slow ultraviolet decrease.

The $O(3)$ sigma-model analysis yielded a mass gap $m\approx0.0019\,\Lambda$ for $g=0.5$, matching qualitative lattice expectations.
