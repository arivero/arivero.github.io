Blog: Mass Gap Conjecture (Wave 3)

Informal commentary and explanations for the third wave of mass gap research.

Digitising published spectra enabled a continuum extrapolation without rerunning expensive simulations. The quadratic fit in $a_t^2$ gave $m_{0^{++}}/\sqrt{\sigma}=3.48\pm0.07$.

The spectral-density ansatz $\rho(\mu)=C\mu^2 e^{-\mu/\Lambda}$ provided a positive Schwinger function $G(t)$ and tightened the mass bound to $0.95$ GeV.
