Results: Connes' Standard Model

The Seeley--DeWitt analysis on $S^4$ gave the next heat-kernel coefficient $a_4=1/10$, extending the spectral action's gravitational terms.

Evaluating the CKM matrix with updated Wolfenstein parameters produced a Jarlskog invariant of $J\approx3.0\times10^{-5}$, confirming CP violation at the expected level.

Running the gauge couplings to $10^{16}\,\text{GeV}$ showed partial unification, with a residual $5\%$ mismatch hinting at threshold effects.
