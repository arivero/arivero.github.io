Blog: Connes' Standard Model

The first wave sets up the noncommutative framework and toys with a supersymmetric extension.

We tallied the 12 generators of the Standard Model gauge group and sketched how squarks and sleptons would appear: 48 new scalar partners in total.

A quick random Yukawa matrix confirmed that full rank couplings are typical, giving distinct masses for each generation.

For deeper background see arXiv:1304.8058.
