  1. Generated a random $3\times3$ Yukawa matrix and evaluated its determinant numerically.

    2. Use `numpy.random.rand(3,3)` and `numpy.linalg.det` to compute $\det Y$.

  2. The determinant $\det Y \approx 0.44$ sets the overall scale for flavor mixing terms.

    3. Interpret this value as fixing the product of eigenvalues in the mass matrix.
