Results: Connes' Standard Model

The heat kernel analysis on the unit four-sphere produced the coefficients $a_0=1/6$ and $a_2=1/3$, matching the expectations of the spectral action and fixing the normalization of gravitational terms.

Implementing a Type-I see-saw mechanism with a Dirac mass of $100$\,GeV and a Majorana scale of $10^{14}$\,GeV yielded a light neutrino mass of $0.1$\,eV, while keeping the heavy state essentially at the Majorana scale.

Using the one-loop QCD beta function $\beta_0=-7$ for six flavours, the running coupling decreases with energy, demonstrating asymptotic freedom consistent with the noncommutative geometry model.
