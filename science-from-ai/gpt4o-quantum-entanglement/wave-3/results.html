Results: Quantum Entanglement

The Bell state violated the CHSH inequality with $\langle B\rangle=2\sqrt2$, matching the Tsirelson bound.

Tracing the $W$ state down to one qubit produced an entropy of $0.918$ bits, reflecting its distributed entanglement.

The partial transpose of a mixed state yielded a negativity of $0.12$, while a separable control returned zero.
