Blog: My Mathmo Interests (Wave 3)

Informal discussion and notes from the third wave of math explorations.

The stationary distribution of the matrix $\begin{pmatrix}0.7&0.3\\0.2&0.8\end{pmatrix}$ is $(0.4,0.6)$, giving intuition for long-run behaviour in probabilistic automata.

Prime gaps up to $10^7$ continue to stay within the heuristic limit $g(p) \le 2\log^2 p$, with the observed largest gap $154$ near $4.6\times10^6$.
