Blog: My Mathmo Interests (Wave 2)

Informal discussion and notes from the second wave of math explorations.

The probabilistic automaton experiment models transitions with stochastic matrices $P_a$ and $P_b$. Multiplying them for "abba" reveals how randomness influences acceptance probability.

Hyperbolic tilings follow growth $N(r) \sim c \lambda^r$; the BFS counts 12, 61 at radii 2 and 3 yield $\lambda\approx4.9$, hinting at exponential expansion inherent to negative curvature.
